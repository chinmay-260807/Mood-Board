Mood Moodboard is a tactical inspiration tool designed to break creative blocks. By combining procedural generation with manual refinement, users can discover unique aesthetic "vibe" combinations for their projects.

## üöÄ How to Use
1.  **Shuffle**: Click the **SHUFFLE VIBES** button to generate a completely new mood.
2.  **Locking**: Love a specific color or emoji? Click/tap it to **Lock** it. Locked elements persist through future shuffles.
3.  **Customization**: 
    *   **Drag & Drop**: Grab elements (color bars or emoji tiles) to reorder the sequence.
    *   **Filtering**: Use the search bar or category pills to influence the emoji pool.
4.  **Export**: Download high-quality PNG or JPG files of your board. The app automatically cleans the UI for professional results.
5.  **History**: Your favorite moods are automatically saved to local storage.
